Camera adapter for Microscope

F mount Nikon adapter

To be able to take pictures using a microscope, an adapter is required to attach your camera to the microscope. This adapter works by transmitting light from the scope to the sensor of the camera (camera does not need lenses for it).

Instructions for printing

[USE THE BLACKEST DEEPEST COLORS YOU HAVE FOR THIS PRINT]

--> Most of the time I get yellowish images from the camera because of high intensity lights from my office. Light can penetrate the yellow PLA quite easy. Use a denser infill or a more adequate color for the print! <--

23mm diameter Microscope adapter for F mount Nikon cameras.

Solidworks model uploaded so you can change the diameter for your microscope.

It is highly suggested to use any kind of support to print this. Don't have to be support material. I printed my support using the same filament.

Cura engine worked best.

If you want me to make for other diameters just ask! I will be glad to help!
